Sustainability

100% renewable electricity

The plant in Schattdorf is entirely powered by renewable energy, thanks to its own solar power system, the purchase of certified hydroelectricity and its participation in a combined heat and power plant as part of a local “Self-Consumption Association” (ZEV).

Datwyler plant in Schattdorf, Switzerland

Sustainability

Plan for solar panels at the Indian plant (2021)

Use of solar panels to generate electricity will help reduce intake from the public power grid. Supply of more than 2’100 MWh of electricity per year. Reduction of CO2 emission by some 1’800 tons per year. Second phase with solar panels on roof of new building (2022) will add another 2’500 MWh of electricitiy and reduce CO2-emissions by another 2’200 tons.

Plan for solar panels at Datwyler plant in Kesurdi, India
